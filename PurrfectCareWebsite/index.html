About Us

Our pets live for the day, the hour, even the moment.

Welcome to Purrfect Care Clinic , where our genuine love for animals and our dedication to building lasting relationships with clients come together. We aim to make each visit to our clinic a positive experience, and our team of dedicated professionals is here to provide the highest quality veterinary care in a friendly environment to ensure a lifetime of health and happiness for your pets.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To provide compassionate and comprehensive veterinary care that prioritizes the well-being and built trust, respect, integrity, happiness of every pet we serve.

Our Vision

To provide personalized care for pets and their families, ensuring that every pet receives the attention and treatment they deserve.

Our Services

Providing our patients with the most complete range of services and the highest quality in Purffect care has always been our top priority at Vets

Preventive Care

Prevention is the key to your pet’s health.

Diagnostic Medicine

Our vets determine your pet’s medical condition using a variety of diagnostic tools we have available.

Outpatient and Inpatient Hospital Care

Treatment of your pet’s diagnosed medical condition can either be done at home or, in more serious cases, while confined in our hospital.

Medicine

In-house lab and advanced imaging aid accurate pet diagnoses.

Pet Export Assistance

Consultation on the regulation and assistance with medical requirements for international pet travel.

After-Hours Emergency Care

Our Los Banos branch accepts after-hour emergency cases from 6:00 pm to 8:00 am.

Therapy and Recovery

We have different programs to increase mobility, reduce pain and promote healing from surgery, injury or chronic conditions.

Well Pet Care

Comprehensive care and counseling for your pet’s health and behavior.

Grooming, Boarding and Retail

Grooming and boarding packages for dogs and cats, and an in-house pet store for all your pet care needs.

Pain Management

We are committed to managing your pets pain for their comfort.
